Why Ayurvedic tea?

Herb-specific function, not generic wellness claims.

Ayurvedic blending pairs each herb with a specific physiological function. That means you know exactly what you're drinking and why.

  • 🌿

    Ashwagandha — adaptogen for stress

    Classified as an adaptogen, Ashwagandha supports the body's stress-response system. It helps regulate cortisol and promotes restful sleep without sedation.

  • 🌱

    Moringa — micronutrient density, immune support

    Moringa leaf is dense in vitamins C, A, and iron. Paired with Tulsi, it supports daily immune function without stimulants.

  • 🟡

    Turmeric — anti-inflammatory, digestive aid

    Curcumin, turmeric's active compound, supports gut lining integrity and reduces inflammatory markers. Ginger amplifies absorption and aids motility.
